Scholarly identity

A transnational bridge scholar operating across Algeria / North Africa, the UK / Western academia, and Qatar / the Gulf — functioning as a knowledge broker who translates concepts across Arabic, English and French and across academic traditions. The distinctive scholarly identity centres on who produces knowledge, whose narratives are legitimised, and how global hierarchies shape the interpretation of sport in Arab and Muslim societies.
